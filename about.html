About QGLegacy

Our Story

QGLegacy was founded by long-time friends Qilot and Ghost, who share a deep passion for gaming. What began as casual conversations grew into a shared goal of creating something impactful. Driven by our vision and determination, we took the plunge into game/web development. The name "QGLegacy" represents our combined identities and our commitment to building a legacy together. We’re thrilled to bring players an experience filled with creativity, fun, and endless possibilities. Thank you for joining us on this journey!

Meet the Developers

Get to know the brilliant minds behind QGLegacy.

Qilot

Qilot has been a lifetime lover of games. Starting at age 3, he fell in love with gaming, which led him into the world of development. A proud anime fan, Naruto is his favorite series. Outside of gaming, he has been in the nursing field for over six years and shares a decade-long relationship with his high school sweetheart. Driven by passion, he transitioned into pursuing his dream in game development with Ghost by his side.

Ghost

Ghost, a self-taught developer, started coding at a young age and has a true passion for problem-solving. He's the mastermind behind QGLegacy's innovative game mechanics and coding challenges. When he's not debugging code, he’s immersed in the world of sci-fi and strategy games. His relentless drive to innovate ensures that QGLegacy continues to bring exciting and engaging gaming experiences to players.
